Our Story

There is food and there is dining... We know the difference....

Steve Bucknanaman
He is an Executive chef of the nec restaurant. His primary role is in managing the overall kitchen. He is also responsible for managing different outlets of nec restaurant. Another role of him is to control kitchen cost and liaising with suppliers and creating the menus.
Mauricio Poltio
He is the Sous-Chef which is the direct assistant of the main chef. He works according to assigned the duties of the head chef. He is responsible for scheduling the kitchen staff. He works in place of the head chef when he is out. His other roles are looking after the cleanliness, he also trains the entire staff.
Michael Symon
He is a Commis Chef, which is a junior member of staff that works under a chef de partie in order to learn the ins and outs of a specific station. His work duties are Kitchen Porter and dish washer His speciality is in Chinese food.
Anthony Bourdain
He is a chef de partie which sometimes is called “line cook”. He is in charge of a particular area of production. He is responsible for the making of the dishes present in the menu. His speciality is in north and south Indian food.
